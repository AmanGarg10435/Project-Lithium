OUR UNIQUE THREE STEP PROCESS
We at Project Lithium recognize the power websites hold, which is why we design and develop so carefully. When creating websites, we follow a three step process. First, we will discuss what you want your website to do, and look at some templates or other websites that you like. Second, we will design and develop three prototype websites for you to consider and give feedback on. From there, we will lean into the elements you like and remove what you don't in order to create a website that is personal, asthetic and utilitarian.
